About us

Get to know us

Built for people who live, work, and move globally

StarRoam was created to simplify how people stay connected while traveling internationally. In a world where mobility has become part of everyday life, traditional roaming services and physical SIM cards continue to create unnecessary friction, high costs, and unreliable experiences. We built StarRoam to remove those barriers and make global connectivity seamless from the moment travelers arrive at their destination.

Our platform provides instant access to eSIM technology across 190+ countries, allowing users to activate mobile data plans digitally without visiting local stores, changing SIM cards, or relying on unsecured public WiFi networks. Whether traveling for business, remote work, tourism, education, or long-term relocation, StarRoam ensures users remain connected wherever their journey takes them.

At the core of StarRoam is a commitment to simplicity, reliability, and accessibility. We focus on delivering a streamlined experience that allows users to browse destinations, activate plans instantly, manage data usage, and maintain stable high-speed connectivity through trusted global carrier partnerships. Every part of the experience is designed to reduce complexity and create confidence while traveling internationally.

Our mission is simple: to make staying connected around the world effortless, reliable, and accessible for everyone.

An eSIM is a built-in digital SIM that allows you to activate a mobile data plan without using a physical SIM card. Instead of visiting a store or swapping SIMs while traveling, you can install your StarRoam eSIM instantly by scanning a QR code or activating it directly from your device.

StarRoam works with most modern smartphones and tablets that support eSIM technology, including newer iPhone models, Samsung Galaxy devices, Google Pixel phones, iPads, and other compatible devices. Before purchasing, you can easily check if your device supports eSIM from your phone settings or through our compatibility guide.

You can activate your StarRoam eSIM before your trip or once you arrive at your destination. Many travelers prefer setting it up before departure so they can connect immediately after landing. The activation process is quick and usually takes only a few minutes to complete.
