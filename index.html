What is an eSIM?s

An eSIM (embedded SIM) is a digital SIM built directly into your device. It allows you to activate a mobile data plan instantly—without inserting a physical SIM card.

An eSIM is a built-in digital SIM that allows you to activate a mobile data plan without using a physical SIM card. Instead of visiting a store or swapping SIMs while traveling, you can install your StarRoam eSIM instantly by scanning a QR code or activating it directly from your device.

StarRoam works with most modern smartphones and tablets that support eSIM technology, including newer iPhone models, Samsung Galaxy devices, Google Pixel phones, iPads, and other compatible devices. Before purchasing, you can easily check if your device supports eSIM from your phone settings or through our compatibility guide.
