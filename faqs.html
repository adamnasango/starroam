General questions

An eSIM is a built-in digital SIM that allows you to activate a mobile data plan without using a physical SIM card. Instead of visiting a store or swapping SIMs while traveling, you can install your StarRoam eSIM instantly by scanning a QR code or activating it directly from your device.

StarRoam works with most modern smartphones and tablets that support eSIM technology, including newer iPhone models, Samsung Galaxy devices, Google Pixel phones, iPads, and other compatible devices. Before purchasing, you can easily check if your device supports eSIM from your phone settings or through our compatibility guide.

You can activate your StarRoam eSIM before your trip or once you arrive at your destination. Many travelers prefer setting it up before departure so they can connect immediately after landing. The activation process is quick and usually takes only a few minutes to complete.

Yes. StarRoam provides coverage in over 190 countries worldwide, including regional plans designed for multi-country travel. This means you can move between destinations without changing SIM cards or purchasing separate mobile plans for each country you visit.

Yes. Your physical SIM card and personal phone number can remain active while using StarRoam eSIM for mobile data. This allows you to continue receiving calls, SMS messages, and verification codes on your primary number while staying connected abroad using StarRoam.
